The **Well Before Kids KN95** is a vertical bifold mask with adjustable earloop straps. It comes in three different sizes for different age ranges. To get the proper fit, it's important to adjust the earloop straps and mold the nose bridge.

---

{{< figure src="/images/wellbefore-sizing.webp" alt="Wellbefore mask sizing guide. Width is measured by the distance between cheek bones. Length is measured by the distance between the middle of the nose and end of the chin" width="400" >}} 

| Size | Width (in) | Length (in) | Ages |
| --- | :---: | :---: | ---: |
| X-Small | 3.75 | 4.35 | 2 - 4 years |
| Small | 4.15 | 4.9 | 5 - 8 years |
| Regular | 4.5 | 5.7 | 9 - 12 years |

---

*Only children ages two and older should wear face coverings such as KN95 masks.*
